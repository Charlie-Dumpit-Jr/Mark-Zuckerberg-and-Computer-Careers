    What is a Video Game Designer?

    A Video Game Designer is a person who designs gameplay, designing the rules and structures of the game. They are responsible for the content and environment of every video games. They may also write code, using several computer programming languages. A video game designer creates the main features of a video game to make it engaging for users. They design the characters in the game, may assist with storyline or goal of the game, and execute the game design to make it more fun for users. Video Game Designers must be able to work as a team. Designers work with a lot of people developing a game.

    There are 3 main Roles of a Video Game Designer:

  • Lead Designer - Ensure team communication, makes large desig decisions, and present outside of the team.
  • Level Designer - Is the person responsible for creating game environment, levels, and missions.
  • Content Designer - designs and balances game rules
